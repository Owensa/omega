Why MINEMY?
Quick Payouts

All payouts are processed within 24 hours of the end of the mining day.

Instant Withdrawals

All withdrawals made on the MINEMY internal wallets are immediately broadcast to the network for approval so there is no internal layer between you and the network from withdrawing your coins outside of the MINEMY network. (tsk. tsk. Coinbase..)

Low Cost

Our region's benefits of no taxes, low electricity costs, and very large infrastructure to start with allow us to pass on the savings to you and keep our contract prices, electricity rates charged to you, and maintenance fees reasonable.

24/7 Uptime

We offer a 100% uptime guarantee. It is always possible that mining hardware may crash, slow down or completely break. In all these cases, our algorithms ensure that other miners are allocated to the relevant pools to fully compensate for possible hashpower loss. This way our users will not lose a second of mining time.

Stability

We have some of the best and brightest technicians on board to keep check of our hardware and ensure they are set up and optimized properly for both maximum performance and minimal energy usage.

Security

All of our sensitive information is stored and hashed using SHA256 algorithm to ensure data is stored securely.
